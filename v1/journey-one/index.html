  • Hedge Hill project outcomes 2021

    Last updated: 20 July 2022 | Levelling Up Fund | 5 MB

    A lack of integrated investment together with out of town retail and residential growth has led to different areas of Hedgehill city centre being less well connected, and major assets like the historic area less well used than it should be. Over the last year residents, businesses and a range of local institutions have worked together, through Hedgehill Connections and Hedgehill city, to set clear priorities and a vision for Hedgehill.
